Privacy Policy

Ensuring your privacy is one of my main priorities. This Privacy Policy document contains details about the types of information that are collected and recorded, as well as how I utilize it. If you have any additional questions or require further information regarding my Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact me. This Privacy Policy is applicable solely to my online activities and is valid for visitors to my website in terms of the information they share and/or collect on this website. Please note that this policy does not apply to any information collected offline or through channels other than this website.

Consent

By using my website, you hereby give your consent to my Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. Your support and understanding of my commitment to safeguarding your privacy are greatly appreciated.

Information Collection and Use

I utilize XAMPP to monitor the performance of my website. When using this tool, all site measurement is carried out in absolute anonymity. No cookies are set, and no personal data is collected. All data is aggregated for statistical purposes only.

If you decide to subscribe to my social media accounts, I will solely thank you.

Use of Collected Information

  • Understanding and analyzing how you use my website: By analyzing certain behaviors and patterns, I gain insights that allow me to optimize the website's functionality and performance, ensuring it meets your needs.
  • Sending you emails: If you have subscribed to my newsletter, you will receive it on a regular basis unless you choose to opt out. I will never communicate for any other reason or pass on your data to anybody.
  • Finding and preventing fraud: The information collected assists in detecting and mitigating fraudulent activities, ensuring the security and integrity of the website and its users.
  • Providing, operating, and maintaining my website: The collected information is essential for the overall provision, operation, and maintenance of the website, enabling its functionality and ensuring a smooth user experience.
  • Improving, personalizing, and expanding my website: The collected information helps me enhance your experience, tailor content to your preferences, and develop and grow my website.

Children's Information

Another priority is to provide protection for children while they use the Internet. I strongly encourage parents and guardians to actively observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their children's online activities. I do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided such information on my website, I urge you to contact me immediately. I will make every effort to promptly remove such information from my records and ensure the privacy and safety of your child.

GDPR Rights

I want to ensure that you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. As a user, you are entitled to certain rights as stated below. If you make a request, I am obliged to respond within one month. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact me.

  • The right to access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request corrections to any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request the completion of any information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure: You have the right to request the erasure of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request the restriction of processing your personal data under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability: You have the right to request the transfer of the data I have collected to another organization or directly to you, under certain conditions.

CCPA Rights

Under the CCPA, among other rights, California consumers have certain rights. If you make a request, I have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of the following rights, please contact me.

  • The right to request that a business, which collects a consumer's personal data, disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal data that have been collected.
  • The right to request the deletion of any personal data about the consumer that has been collected by a business.
  • The right to request that a business, which sells a consumer's personal data, does not sell their personal data.
